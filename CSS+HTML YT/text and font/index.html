What is Blockchain Technology and How Does It Work?

The blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across multiple computers. It ensures the security and transparency of data, making it nearly impossible to alter or hack. By using cryptographic techniques, the blockchain enables trustless interactions and eliminates the need for intermediaries. This technology has revolutionized various industries, including finance, supply chain management, and healthcare, by providing a secure and efficient way to store and share information.
